Fun Facts About Pittsburgh

  1. Pittsburgh has more bridges than any other city in the world.
  2. The emoticon was invented in Pittsburgh in 1980 by Carnegie Mellon University computer scientist Scott Fahlman.
  3. The Polio vaccine was created by Dr. Jonas Salk in Pittsburgh in 1950.
  4. The Big Mac was invented in the Pittsburgh suburbs by Jim Delligatti, a local McDonald’s franchisee.
  5. Opened in 1895, the Carnegie Museum of Art was the world’s first Modern art museum.
