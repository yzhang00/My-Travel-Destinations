Fun Facts About Seattle

  1. Seattle is one of America's most educated cities.
  2. Seattle is the number one city for bicyclists in the country.
  3. Seattle is home to the country's first 24/7 radio station.
  4. There are more dogs and cats than children in Seattle.
  5. Seattle was the first major American city to elect a female mayor.
